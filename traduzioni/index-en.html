Once you arrive you will find this gate where on the right side you will find the safe containing the keys.
The code is "0090".

Separate collection of garbage

You will absolutely have to carry out separate collection.
The waste must be exposed on public land between 22:00 the night before and 6:00 the morning of collection, you will have to leave the bins with the correct garbage outside the driveway, near the roadside, and the ecological operators will pass by in the morning to collect the garbage.
When the operators have finished picking up the garbage, you can take the basket home.
At check out you will have to take out all the garbage (which will not be picked up by the ecological operators) and you will have to go to the local ecological center whose location we provide you with.
