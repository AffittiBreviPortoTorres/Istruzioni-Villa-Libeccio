Villa rules

We welcome you and wish you a pleasant stay in our beautiful Villa Libeccio.
We kindly ask you to give the villa the same care and attention as you would to your home and to treat the surrounding environment and the equipment provided with the utmost respect. Here are some information and rules that we ask you to respect during your stay.




  1. During the rental period, you will be solely responsible for the property and its internal and external assets (including deck chairs, umbrellas and other outdoor furniture). So please take care of the property and outdoor furniture, especially in case of bad weather (in case of strong wind block the windows and close the umbrellas) not to place the sun beds on the lawn and to take care of the irrigation system .

  2. The security deposit must be paid upon check-in. At check-out, a member of Paradiso Sardegna in the presence of the guest will make a thorough check to verify the good condition of the property. Paradiso Sardegna reserves the right to carry out a further check on the property after the guest's departure. In the event that damage or anomalies are found (escaped the first check) Paradiso Sardegna will contact you within 48 hours after departure.

  3. Please do not throw any type of solid waste in the kitchen sink drain, so as not to compromise correct operation.

  4. Tap water is not drinkable, but it can be used for cooking.

  5. In case of strong wind, lock the windows and make sure to close the awning.

  6. Separate collection: In respect of our beautiful island, we kindly ask you to divide the waste into separate bags as follows and to hand it over as indicated in the calendar provided.

  7. Smoking is not allowed inside the Villa, but only in the outdoor areas, taking care not to damage the furnishings.

  8. Before using the pool it is necessary to rinse with the special shower.

  9. It is not allowed to use mats on the lawn.
